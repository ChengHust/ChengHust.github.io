Dr. Cheng He is currently a Research Assistant Professor with the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Southern University of Science and Technology, China. He serves as a Publication Chair for the12th International Conference on Bio-inspired Computing: Theories and Applications (2017), and a Co-Chair for Competition on Online Data-Driven Multi-Objective Optimization (2019), and a Co-Chair for IEEE Symposium on Model-Based Evolutionary Algorithms (2019 and 2020). His main research interests are Artificial/Computational Intelligence (including evolutionary multi-objective optimization, model-based optimization, large-scale optimization, etc.).
