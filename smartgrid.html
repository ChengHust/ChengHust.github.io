IEEE CIS Task Force on Smart Grid

IEEE CIS Task Force on Smart Grid

IEEE Computational Intelligence Society — Intelligent Systems Applications Technical Committee

This Task Force serves as a hub for researchers and practitioners working at the intersection of computational intelligence and smart grid technologies. We focus on artificial intelligence, evolutionary computation, multi-objective optimization, and machine learning applied to power system measurement, condition monitoring, and grid intelligence. The Task Force organizes competitions, special sessions, and collaborative research under the IEEE CIS umbrella.
