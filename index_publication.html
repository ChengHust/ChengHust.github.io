Dr. Cheng He is currently a Research Associate with the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Huazhong University of Science and Technology, China. His main research interests are Artificial/Computational Intelligence (includes evolutionary multi-objective optimization, model-based optimization, large-scale optimization, etc.) and its applications.
